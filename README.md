# Vantage

**Engineered for Results.**

The official website for Vantage — a full-spectrum firm delivering integrated solutions across contracting, interiors, credit restoration, business development, creative design, and software/app development.

## Divisions

- **Vantage Build** — Contracting & Construction
- **Vantage Interiors** — Interior Planning & Decorating
- **Vantage Credit** — Credit Restoration & Financial Strategy
- **Vantage Ventures** — Business Development & Management
- **Vantage Design** — Creative Design & Branding
- **Vantage Tech** — Software & App Development

## Founder

Marlowe Greene — Founder & Principal. 20+ years in master contracting, 10+ years in business development and credit strategy. Serial entrepreneur with a cross-domain specialization.

## Stack

- Static HTML/CSS/JS (no framework)
- Hosted on GitHub Pages
- Form handling via Formspree
- Google Fonts: Cormorant Garamond, DM Sans, Space Mono

## Structure

```
/
├── index.html          — Homepage
├── about.html          — Founder bio + brand story
├── services.html       — Six divisions detailed
├── portfolio.html      — Case studies & project showcase
├── contact.html        — Contact form
├── quote.html          — Quote request form
├── thank-you.html      — Form submission confirmation
├── 404.html            — Error page
├── style.css           — Global stylesheet
├── logo.png            — Primary logo (transparent)
├── mark.png            — Chevron mark only
├── favicon.ico         — Browser tab icon
├── sitemap.xml         — Search engine map
├── .nojekyll           — GitHub Pages config
└── google*.html        — Google Search Console verification (DO NOT DELETE)
```

## Development Notes

- Portfolio images are placeholder cards pending client-supplied media.
- Founder photo on `about.html` displays Vantage logo until headshot is provided.
