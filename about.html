The Founder

MarloweGreene.

Founder & Principal · Vantage

Marlowe Greene is not a generalist. He is a cross-domain specialist — a distinction that separates Vantage from every other firm in the market.

With more than two decades of master contracting experience and over a decade of dedicated work in business development, credit strategy, and entrepreneurial consulting, Marlowe has built a career defined by one operating principle: the customer's outcome is the only outcome that matters.

As a serial entrepreneur, Marlowe has launched and scaled multiple ventures across construction, design, and professional services. He brings the perspective of an owner — not an employee — to every client engagement.

Vantage is the culmination of that experience: a firm architectured to deliver the full spectrum of what growth-oriented clients need, without forcing them to manage five different vendors with five different standards.
